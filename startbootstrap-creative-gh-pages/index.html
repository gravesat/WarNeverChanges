Why does the location of war matter?

An anti-war campaign for the prurposes of a university module. This campaign centres on the idea that people are unconcerned with current warsthat they are not geographically close to.

Key Points

Location

War Never Changes was founded in 2017 by university students Hayleigh Phillips, Charlie Bevan and Talitha Gravesande. They were shocked and horrified by the violence taking place across the world and how their own countries were not helping.

Domestic

Our current campaign is 'Why Does Location Matter?'. This campaign was created to emphasis to people how the location of a war should not matter. That no matter where the war is, it should be taken seriously by everyone around the world.

Justification

We aim to raise awareness of wars around the world that mainstream news outlets do not cover, and to educate first-world countries on a war they may not know about.

Social

There are many ways for you to get involved and support our cause. We can be even stronger when we're together.
